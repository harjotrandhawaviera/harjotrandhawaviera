Notes on Photos

When taking a full body picture, it is important that you are pictured from head to toe.
  • Ideally neutral clothing.
  • If there is a significant change in appearance, please update the pictures immediately.
  • All photos in color.
  • Only photos where you are alone.
  • No selfies or photos in front of the mirror, these look very unprofessional and are usually of poor quality.
  • No heavily edited photos, Photoshop and Co. are undesirable.
  • Be careful with permissiveness, do not upload too permissive pictures, otherwise it looks unserious.
  • No party or group pictures, no alcoholic drinks, cigarettes, etc.
  • No hats, sunglasses or other things that cover the face.
When using professional pictures:
  • Only upload photos that do not violate copyright laws.
  • Permission must be obtained from the photographer.
