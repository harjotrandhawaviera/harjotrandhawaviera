COOKIES

In order to make best visitor experience to our website and to enable the use of certain functions, we use so-called cookies on various pages. These are small text files that are stored on your terminal device. Some of the cookies we use are deleted after the end of the browser session, i.e. after you close your browser (so-called session cookies). Other cookies remain on your device and enable us or our partner companies to recognize your browser on your next visit (persistent cookies). You can set your browser so that you are informed about the setting of cookies and decide individually about their acceptance or non-acceptance of cookies for certain cases or in general. In case of non-acceptance of cookies, the functionality of our website may be limited.
