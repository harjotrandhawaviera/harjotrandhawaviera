When am I liable for sales tax?

  • In principle, every entrepreneur is liable to pay sales tax
  • Invoices of small entrepreneurs may not show sales tax, a corresponding legal notice is required
  • for the small business regulation, the following limits may not be exceeded: 22,000 Euros in the previous or current fiscal year and 50,000 Euros in the following year
  • entrepreneurial obligation to control: if the limits are exceeded, the VAT obligation automatically takes effect in the following year (the tax office will not draw attention to this separately)
