When am I liable for sales tax (VAT)?

  • In principle, every entrepreneur is liable to pay sales tax (VAT)
  • Invoices of small entrepreneurs may not show sales tax (VAT), a corresponding legal notice is indispensable (ยง 19 UStG)
  • For the small business regulation the following limits may not be exceeded: 22,000 Euro in the previous or current business year and 50,000 Euro in the following year
  • Entrepreneurial duty to control: if the limits are exceeded, the VAT liability automatically takes effect in the following year (the tax office does not have to draw attention to this separately)
