Notes on the test

In the next step you will be shown the test questions with possible answers.

  • Please read the question carefully and then select one or more answers off.
  • With one click on the 'Next'-button you will get to the next question.
  • With a click on the 'Back'-Button, you can return to the previous question.

After the last question you will see a summary of the test with all questions and your answers.
Here you can check your answers again and correct them if necessary.

Finally, please send your answers to us by clicking on the 'Send answers'- Button.

The result will be displayed immediately.
If you have not passed the test, you can repeat it without any problems.

Good luck!
